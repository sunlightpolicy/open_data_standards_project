Many city challenges are actually region-wide challenges. Yet the lack of usage of standards for many datasets means that it is often time-consuming to do something as simple as making a map of a metropolitan area showing data seamlessly across jurisdictions. For this project, I created an example of a map showing business licenses for both Kansas City and Independence, Missouri:


Of course, this concept isn’t limited to business licenses — in fact, that's probably not even the best use case, it's just the example used here. There could be regional maps of everything from 911 calls to building permits.
